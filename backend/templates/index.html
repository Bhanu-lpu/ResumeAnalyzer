    Pro Tips for Writing a Job-Winning Resume

    Did you know that most recruiters spend less than 10 seconds reading a resume? To stand out, keep your resume clear, concise, and relevant. Use strong action verbs, include measurable achievements, and customize your resume for every job application. Avoid long paragraphs — instead, use bullet points for skills and experiences. A well-formatted resume not only makes you look professional but also helps Applicant Tracking Systems (ATS) recognize your skills more effectively.
